Brinstar

Brinstar 1

Found in: Spore Spawn Super Room

Commonly referred to as Spore Spawn Supers. Normally a reward for defeating Spore Spawn, this pack can be acquired without ever fighting Spore Spawn, which is useful in 100% Item runs.

Brinstar 2

Found in: Early Supers Room

Commonly referred to as Early Supers. It is intended for the player to use Speed Booster to acquire this pack by outrunning the shutters, but by performing a mockball, this pack can be acquired without Speed Booster and before Spore Spawn Supers, making it the first obtainable Super Missile pack.
